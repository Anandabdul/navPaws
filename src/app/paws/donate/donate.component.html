DONATE TO NAVPAWS

Thank you for considering a donation to navPaws! Your contributions help us provide a safe and loving environment for rescued animals. We accept a variety of items to meet the diverse needs of our furry friends. Here's how you can help:

Used Items

  • Old Newspapers: Great for lining kennels and cages.
  • Used Towels: Perfect for bathing and drying our animals.
  • Bedsheets and Tarpaulins: Provide warmth and comfort, and are useful for creating temporary shelters.

Sanitation Supplies

  • Dettol: Essential for maintaining a clean and hygienic environment.
  • Dog Collars: Ensure the safety and identification of our dogs.
  • Surgical Masks and Gloves: Important for the health and safety of our staff and volunteers.

Nutrition Supplies

  • Rice and Wheat: Staple foods for our animals' diets.
  • Bird Feed: To keep our feathered friends healthy and happy.
  • Pedigree: High-quality food for our dogs.
  • Marie Biscuits: A favorite treat for many of our animals.
  • Cat Foods: Nutritional meals for our feline residents.

Cash Donations

Your monetary donations are crucial in helping us cover medical expenses, shelter maintenance, and other operational costs. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact on the lives of the animals we rescue.
Your donations make a significant difference in the lives of the animals we rescue. Thank you for your generosity and support!
