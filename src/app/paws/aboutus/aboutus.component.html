Welcome to NavPaws Animal Rescue!

At NavPaws Animal Rescue, our mission is to give every animal a second chance at a happy, healthy life. We are a dedicated team of animal lovers and professionals who work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.

Our Story

NavPaws was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate volunteers who saw a critical need for more comprehensive animal rescue services in our community. What started as a small operation has grown into a vibrant network of volunteers, foster homes, and supporters, all united by a common goal: to save lives and create a world where every animal is valued and cared for.

What We Do

Rescue

We rescue animals from a variety of situations, including those abandoned, abused, or surrendered by their owners. Our rescue efforts are not limited to cats and dogs; we extend our services to all types of animals in need.

Rehabilitation

Once an animal is in our care, we provide necessary medical treatment, behavioral training, and plenty of love and attention to help them recover and thrive. Our team works closely with veterinarians and animal behaviorists to ensure each animal gets the best possible care.

Rehoming

Our ultimate goal is to find permanent, loving homes for the animals in our care. We carefully screen potential adopters and match them with animals that fit their lifestyle and environment. We also provide ongoing support to ensure successful, long-term adoptions.

Our Team

Our team is made up of dedicated volunteers, foster families, and animal care professionals. Each member brings their unique skills and unwavering compassion to our organization, making it possible to provide exceptional care and support to every animal we rescue.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to get involved with NavPaws Animal Rescue:

  • Adopt: Give a loving home to one of our rescued animals.
  • Foster: Provide temporary care for animals in need while they wait for their forever homes.
  • Volunteer: Help with daily operations, events, and animal care.
  • Donate: Support our rescue efforts with a financial contribution or supplies.
